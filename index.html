mission statement:

abcpvd (Art Book Collective, Providence) is a collective of artists who create interactive artwork and art spaces where we invite people to play together.

We invite viewers to touch our work, page through books, transform sculptures, join choruses, play games, learn, meet new friends, and be empowered to make participatory art themselves.

abcpvd is a non-hierarchical collective that is committed to making accessible artwork through affordability and considerate distribution. We source responsibly through practices like tool lending and material reuse. We work to make our spaces diverse and inclusive.

Follow us on instagram @artbookpvd or sign up for our mailing list to stay updated on our events. Reach us at artbookcollectivepvd@gmail.com to inquire about event planning, commissions, and collaborations.
